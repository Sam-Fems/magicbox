About Us

At MagicBox Gift Concierge, we're passionate about creating unforgettable gifting experiences. Our journey began with a challenge faced by our founder, Fairy Oyin. She discovered the lack of structure around curated gift services and turned it into an opportunity to simplify thoughtful gift-giving.

We help you find the perfect gift for any occasion — clients, events, or loved ones. Our goal is to remove the stress and deliver joy, love, and connection through gifts.

Our Approach

Partnering with over 200 local artisans, we empower creators and promote sustainable growth. We align with SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being) and SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth).
